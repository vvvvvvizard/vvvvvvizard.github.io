Certification Overview

The AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate certification demonstrates the ability to design and deploy scalable, highly available, and fault-tolerant systems on AWS.

Key Knowledge Areas

1. Designing Resilient Architectures

  • Multi-tier architecture design
  • High availability and fault tolerance
  • Disaster recovery strategies
  • Scalability and elasticity

2. Designing High-Performing Architectures

  • Storage solutions for performance
  • Compute and database optimization
  • Caching strategies
  • Content delivery networks

3. Designing Secure Applications and Architectures

  • Identity and access management
  • Data protection mechanisms
  • Infrastructure protection
  • Incident response

4. Cost Optimization

  • Cost-effective storage solutions
  • Compute and database cost optimization
  • Cost monitoring and control
  • Right-sizing resources

Exam Details

  • Format: Multiple choice and multiple response
  • Duration: 130 minutes
  • Cost: $150 USD
  • Languages: English, Japanese, Korean, Simplified Chinese
