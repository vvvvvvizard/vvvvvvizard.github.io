Cryptographic Computing

Collaborate without compromising privacy

Secure Data Collaboration

Imagine your company and many other companies each have your own secret recipe (dataset) that you want to keep private. However, you both want to combine your recipes in a way that helps you create a new dish (output Y) without revealing your ingredients to each other.

How It Works

1. Encrypt Your Data

Each party encrypts their private dataset using advanced cryptographic techniques, turning it into a secure code that cannot be understood on its own.

2. Secure Computation

Special cryptographic tools allow computations to be performed directly on the encrypted data, combining information from multiple sources while maintaining privacy.

3. Generate Insights

The system produces valuable outputs and insights without ever revealing the original private data, enabling collaborative innovation while protecting trade secrets.

Applications

  • Collaborative AI model training
  • Cross-company data analysis
  • Secure financial computations
  • Healthcare research with privacy
